1. Open up your desktop browser and go to bookmark manager. Export the bookmarks to HTML format and save the file to your computer.
  2. Connect your iOS device (iPhone, iPad, etc) to the computer and open iTunes.
  3. In iTunes, click on the desired device from the left pane under the "Devices" section.
  4. Go to "Apps" tab using the top navigation. Scroll to the bottom of the page to the "File Sharing" section.
  5. Click on the app from the list and click the "Add..." button. Select the file you saved in step 1 (ensure it is an HTML file).
  6. Sync your device with iTunes by clicking the "Sync" button at the bottom right of the iTunes screen.
  7. Done! Next time your open browser app on your device, your bookmarks will be loaded in.
